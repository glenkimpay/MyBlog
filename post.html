Dance to Express not to Impress

Dance is not just a dance. It defines me.



Dance to express and not to impress is not just a statement,
It is a belief that I follow.
I want to grow stronger in my character,
To improve my skills not to prove people that I’m strong
But to prove myself that I can make a difference,
And I’m doing it because I love to do it.

It is a freedom of expression where you can be who you are and nobody can judge you.
It has different styles based on the person’s preference.
With the different genres to choose from.
Having said all that I believe that no matter how good you are,
No matter how difficult your choreography is,

“If you don’t do it with your passion, then it will be just like a food with no taste in it.”

It may look good but if you don’t feel the dancer’s heart,
It’s totally useless.
I’ve met a lot of dancers in the Baguio Dance Community.
I’ve learned that the most humble persons in the dance community
Are the ones who actually made started from the bottom up now here.
And the ones that does not, are the ones who fall.

You Are My Brother and You Are My Bestfriend

"A friend loves at all times and a brother is born for Adversity" - Proverbs 17:17


You say, you’re not loved and don’t deserve to receive a letter,
I don’t want you to feel that way, I want you to feel better,
Because, not only you are my Bestfriend, you are my Brother,
I’ll try if you want me to write you a million letter,
If that’s what it takes to make you feel better,

So Brother, when you’re feeling like nobody cares just read this poem, and know that I’ll always be there.

Whenever you feel sad and that no one is around,
Always know I will be around.
When you think that nobody care
I hope you know that I will always care.
When you feel like no one is by your side
I’ll be there even if the tide is high

So don’t let me catch you saying, you have no one and that no one is around

Wake up!! Get out of this haze,
I’ll always be your friend forever and always.

Remember, You’re special, you’re strong, loving and kind,
Know matter who’s there you’ll speak your mind,
A friend like you is hard to find,
One that touches you deep inside.
You’ve given me strength to carry on,
You’ve offered your hand to hold on,
When times are tough, I know you’re there,
To offer support and show you care.
I not for you I would have drowned,
But you help me keep on solid ground.
I believe you were sent from the man above,
Because he knows the strength of your love.
You show that love in so many ways,
It helps me get through my darkest of days.
So for that I write this poem for you,
And tell you from my heart THANK YOU!!!
Pull this paper out, and read this poem over, and over again,
Because you are my Brother and you are my Bestfriend.
